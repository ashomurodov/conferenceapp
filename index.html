ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

International Scientific Conference "Construction the formation of living environment" will be held from December 2 to December 3, 2023 at "Tashkent State Transport University.

The main aim of the Conference is to provide the sustainable innovative development of the transport industry, to preserve the continuity of scientific schools, to form a new level of training professional staff, to strengthen scientific and business contacts

The language of the Conference is English.

Participation format:

Participants can to make their report in two formats: an oral presentation on the days of the conference (including online presentation) or recording a video presentation.
